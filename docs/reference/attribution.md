### Academic Foundations

**Wicked Problems Concept:**
- Rittel, H. W. J., & Webber, M. M. (1973)
- "Dilemmas in a general theory of planning"
- Policy Sciences, 4(2), 155-169
- 10 defining characteristics of wicked problems

**Super-Wicked Problems Concept:**
- Levin, K., Cashore, B., Bernstein, S., & Auld, G. (2012)
- "Overcoming the tragedy of super wicked problems"
- Policy Sciences, 45(2), 123-152
- 4 additional characteristics beyond wicked
